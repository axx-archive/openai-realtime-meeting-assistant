**Design law:** docs/reskin-standing-requirements.md is binding. Glass & ink v2: monochrome `--accent`, `--live #30D158` for presence, `--ember #FF6B4A` earned-only, Google Sans Flex for person text, lowercase Geist Mono for system text, glass chrome tokens, radii 12/16/22/28/full, press .97, no emoji, no scaffolding copy.

**Hard rules for every implementer:**
- Shared working tree: NEVER `git checkout/reset/stash/clean/revert`; additive-only; don't touch untracked `design-system/`; no commit/push/deploy (orchestrator does).

## 1. Decisions (with rationale)

### D1 — The lobby: SHIP on the room surface. The rail's room tool becomes the lobby.

The working hypothesis holds, with one amendment: **the lobby is selection-first, not list-first.** The current `renderRoomLandingState` void (recon-08) wastes the entire pre-join surface while ALL management crowds the Morning-Brief home (recon-02). Relocating list + management to the room tool fixes both — but a bare list would tax the 95% case (walking into your own office).

**Resolution of the office-fast-path tension:** the lobby is a **hero + rail-list** composition. The selected room (default: office; or the `?room=` boot param / home deep-link target) renders as a full hero with ONE primary join button. Click count for the office case is unchanged from today: rail room tool → join. Browsing other rooms is one extra click (select in the list, then join) — correct priority. No auto-join on selection, ever: selection is browsing, join is consent (this also serves the green room's "no camera light while browsing" rule).

Management (create, invite/links, passcode, archive) lives on the lobby list rows behind a real popover menu — not on home, not inline bare-text strips.

### D2 — Home: SHIP the slim live-now strip; the management card leaves home.

Home is Morning-Brief territory; the recon confirms the dilution (recon-02: create form + mint + archive above the brief). The full card is replaced by a glanceable strip: **only rooms with people inside**, green `--live` dot + name + `n inside` mono, click → lobby with that room selected. Quiet state = one compact row ("rooms · all quiet") that opens the lobby — no list, no chrome. The live-now signal (the one thing the recon praised) is kept and promoted; everything else moves to the lobby. Also fixes the fresh-login hydration hole (login success path never calls `loadRoomsList()` — index.html ~24943).

### D3 — In-call invite: SHIP in the meeting bar, members only, as a unified Invite popover.

The real moment you want a link is mid-meeting, and today there is NO in-call guest mint at all — while the topbar "Copy link" copies a member-only `/?room=` URL with nothing saying so (ambiguity trap). Decision: one **Invite** button (person-plus) in the member meeting bar opens a glass popover with two clearly-labeled actions: *for teammates* (copy `/?room=` link) and *for guests* (mint one-time `/g#` link, shown once with copy + expiry note). The topbar `#copyRoomLink` is retired; its slot instead displays **the room's name** (fixing "the room name appears nowhere in-call", recon-11). Guests never see Invite: it renders inside the member-only meeting-bar group that `is-guest` CSS already hides, pinned by test.

### D4 — Office invite policy: ALLOW with explicit consent + persistent visibility + revoke path.

Not disabled (AJ's standing product call keeps office minting allowed) and not silent (the recon proves the latch is invisible everywhere — unacceptable for a permanent side effect on office intelligence). Shape:
- **At mint (office only):** the Invite popover swaps to a confirm step before minting: *"inviting a guest quiets scout. while any office guest link lives, office sittings are listen-only — the record still builds (transcripts, memory, digests), but scout won't speak or act on its own. this lasts until every office link expires or is revoked."* → `[mint the link]` / `[cancel]`. Mono voice, `--warn`-soft tone. Non-office rooms mint without the interstitial.
- **While any office link lives:** the lobby office row + hero wear a `listen-only · guest link live` badge (mono, warn-soft) with a *manage links* affordance. Requires one honest server field: `guestLinkActive` on `GET /rooms` rows (any unexpired, unrevoked link) — `guestEnabled` alone can't say this, since the latch is computed from live links (§7.1 of the rooms spec).
- **Revoke ships:** the lobby ⋯ menu gains a *guest links* panel over the EXISTING `GET /rooms/{id}/guest-links` + `.../revoke` endpoints (mint-only today; fire-and-forget gap closed). Copy is honest about the known limitation: *"revoking stops new joins; guests already inside stay until they leave."*

### D5 — Green room: SHIP, as the join seam itself (lobby hero for members, the gate for guests).

Fresh call, never shipped, and the answer is yes: it is Zoom/Meet table stakes for the outsider's first impression, the raw material is already live (the looks/blur preview pipeline in settings acquires an on-demand preview camera and releases it — `startVideoLookPreview`/`stopVideoLookPreview`, index.html ~32008/~32108), and it structurally fixes two broken flows at once (the getUserMedia join wedge and the camera-hot-on-join guest gate). Shape:
- **Not a separate screen.** A green-room block inside the lobby hero (members) and inside the guest name gate (guests). No modal, no extra step — join stays one click.
- **Media only on explicit intent:** browsing the lobby never lights the camera. The preview tile idles as dark glass with a "check your camera" affordance; tapping it (or switching the cam toggle on at the guest gate) acquires a preview-only stream through the existing looks pipeline. Preview stops + releases on join, on deselect, and on leaving the surface.
- **Toggles carry into join:** mic/cam on-off chips, persisted per device (`localStorage bonfire:joinAV`), applied by `joinRoom` right after track acquisition via the existing mute machinery (`track.enabled=false` + meeting-bar button state) — join never blocks on the preview.
- **Looks surface pre-join:** the existing `.video-look-chip[data-look]` row (none/warm/crisp/mono/low-light/blur bg) renders compactly under the preview, driving the same persisted look state the in-call pipeline reads. The buried settings picker stays; this is a second door to the same state.
- **Watchdogs kill the wedge class:** preview acquire races an 8s timeout ("camera didn't answer — you can join with it off"); the join path acquires media BEFORE dialing the websocket with a 12s timeout and an honest failure state — no more `is-room-entering` forever, no more phantom "live · 1 inside" seat (the seat is only taken after media resolves).
- **Guests:** the gate becomes name + AV check + join in one screen, and finally names the room ("you're invited to {room}") via a new rate-limited `POST /guest/lookup {token}` (token holders only — the token is already the capability; no new oracle). "Use Face ID" is demoted to a quiet text link ("joining as a member? sign in").

## 3. Per-surface component specs

Real anchors are given as `function` / `#id` / `~line` in index.html as of `b013c6b`-lineage tree. All new class names use the `lobby__*`, `greenroom__*`, `invite-pop__*`, `home-live__*` prefixes.

### 3.1 Lobby (room tool, `[data-tool="room"]:not(.is-in-room)`)

Replaces the `#roomNotConnected` void (`.room-empty`, markup ~20770-20773, CSS ~14057, renderer `renderRoomLandingState` ~49920).

**Layout — desktop (≥960px):** the black stage keeps its full-bleed identity (the room "is a stage"); the lobby floats on it as two glass regions: a centered hero column (max-width 520px) and a right-side rooms rail (280px, `--glass-panel`, scrolls independently). **Mobile:** single column — hero first, list beneath, `+ new room` last; the hero join button full-width.

**Hero (selected room):**
- Title: room name in Google Sans Flex (person-scale, like today's `.room-empty__title`), quiet koan meta beneath in mono: occupied → `live now · {n} inside`; empty → `memory starts when the room speaks.`
- Badge row (mono, 12px, single line): `passcode` lock glyph · `guests` glyph · office-only `listen-only · guest link live` (warn-soft) when `guestLinkActive` — replaces the 10px second-line glyph soup (recon-06 polish issue).
- **Passcode gate:** if `passcodeRequired` and no verified passcode this session, an inline field + `join` sits where the join button would be. Wrong passcode: the `access_denied reason=passcode` seam (`handleAccessDenied` ~35996, case ~33975) must land HERE — field shakes (one 300ms x-translate), mono error line `wrong passcode.` below it, field cleared and refocused. Never a silent close (recon-35/36 broken issue: feedback currently goes to the invisible meeting-bar `setLog`).
- **Green room block** — see §3.2.
- **Join button:** today's `.room-empty__join` white pill, one per lobby, always enabled when a joinable room is selected; `connecting…` while `is-room-entering` (existing `syncRoomEntryAffordance` ~30267); on media-timeout failure flips to `try again` with an error line (see §3.6).

**Rooms rail list:** one row per unarchived room: `--live` green dot (presence = green per law; never ember), name (sans), `n inside` (mono, only when live), lock/guest glyphs inline at 12px, ⋯ button. Selected row carries `aria-current` + `--line-2` left rule. Clicking a row selects (`activeJoin = {roomId, passcode:''}` → `renderLobby()`) — **never joins.** The row for the room you're currently seated in (possible when visiting the lobby mid-call? no — lobby only renders `:not(.is-in-room)`) is not a case. `+ new room` footer button opens the create form inline at the list bottom (name / passcode optional / allow guests / create) — form closes on success, on outside click, and on Escape.
- Empty-ish state: office always exists, so the list never renders empty. No filler copy.

**⋯ popover (per-room management)** — replaces `toggleRoomsRowActions` (~50052) inline strips:
- A real glass popover (`--glass-chrome`, r16, `--glass-blur`, shadow tokens), anchored to the ⋯, dismissed on outside click, Escape, and tool switch. One open at a time. Items are proper rows with hover chrome, mono labels: `invite a guest`, `guest links ({activeCount})`, `set passcode`/`change passcode`, `archive` (non-office only; office shows no archive row).
- `invite a guest` → mint flow (§3.4 shared component; office confirm interstitial per D4).
- `guest links` → panel listing each link (label or `link {id}`, `created {rel}`, `expires {rel}`, revoked state) from `GET /rooms/{id}/guest-links`, each with a `revoke` action → `POST .../guest-links/revoke {id}` → list refresh + `loadRoomsList()`. Footer note (mono, `--text-3`): `revoking stops new joins; guests already inside stay until they leave.`
- `archive` → occupant-aware confirm (§3.7).

**Hydration:** `loadRoomsList()` (~49956) gains call sites in the login-success fan-out (~24943, next to `loadScoutChatThreads()`) and the passkey success path (~25008 region) — fixing the broken fresh-login empty state (recon-01). `renderLobby()` (successor of `renderRoomLandingState`) is called from the same seams that call it today (~49824, ~49916, ~49953, ~49981) plus room-tool activation.

### 3.2 Green room (shared block: lobby hero + guest gate)

- **Preview tile:** 16:9, r16, `--surface-2`; idle state shows a camera glyph + mono `check your camera` (button semantics, whole tile pressable). On tap: acquire via the EXISTING preview machinery — reuse `startVideoLookPreview` (~32008) generalized to accept a target `<video>` (it already handles preview-only `getUserMedia`, the real shader pipeline, CSS-filter fallback, and full release in `stopVideoLookPreview` ~32108). Acquire races an **8s timeout**; on timeout/denial the tile shows mono `camera didn't answer — you can join with it off.` and the cam chip flips off. Mirror the self-view (`transform: scaleX(-1)`, matching the in-call self tile).
- **Mic/cam chips:** two toggle chips under the tile (mic glyph, cam glyph; `aria-pressed`; off = strikethrough glyph + `--text-3`). State persisted to `localStorage['bonfire:joinAV']` (`{mic:true,cam:true}` default). Toggling cam ON while the tile is idle also starts the preview (explicit intent). Toggling cam OFF stops the preview track but keeps the tile mounted with `camera off` mono.
- **Looks row:** compact clone of the settings chips (`data-look` values, ~21873-21878) driving the same persisted look state (`videoLookSettingsValue()` and its setter), so the in-call pipeline picks the choice up with zero new plumbing. Sliders do NOT come along — the full picker stays in settings; a `fine-tune in settings` text link is enough.
- **Join carry:** `joinRoom` (~30283) reads `bonfire:joinAV` after `createLocalMediaStream` resolves (~30792) and applies `track.enabled=false` + syncs the meeting-bar mute buttons through the existing toggle functions. Tracks are still acquired when off (fast unmute mid-call; matches the shipped mute model). The green-room preview is stopped (fully released) before acquisition so it never fights the join for the device — the same discipline `startVideoLookPreview` already uses in-call (clone-don't-reacquire).
- **Teardown:** preview stops on join, on room deselect, on tool switch away, and on guest-gate unmount. Never keep a hot camera on a surface the user left.

### 3.3 Home live-now strip (office tool)

Replaces `#officeRoomsCard` (~20484-20496) and its JS (`renderRoomsList`/`roomsListRow`/create/mint/passcode/archive handlers ~49984-50220 all relocate to the lobby).
- **Live state:** one `office-home-card`-style glass row per live room (cap 4, newest-live first): green `--live` dot (breathing per motion law only while that room is live), room name (sans), `` {n} inside `` (mono), chevron. Click → `setActiveTool('room')` with `activeJoin.roomId` preselected. No Open button, no ⋯, no create, no mint — zero management on home.
- **Quiet state:** a single compact row: dot (grey) + `rooms` + mono `all quiet` + chevron → opens the lobby. (One honest row beats hiding — the lobby needs a discoverable door from home; no explanatory subtitle per the copy law.)
- Renders from the same `roomsList` snapshot; updates on the `rooms` office event via `handleRoomsSnapshot`.

### 3.4 Invite popover (shared: meeting bar in-call + lobby ⋯ menu)

One component, two mounts.
- **Trigger (in-call):** new `#inviteToggle` button (person-plus glyph, label `Invite` at desktop widths) in the member meeting bar next to `Send notes` (markup region ~21474-21513). It sits INSIDE the member-only control group that `is-guest` CSS hides (member-only rule block ~433) — pinned by test. Mobile member bar: icon-only, same popover as a bottom sheet.
- **Popover body (glass, r16, outside-click + Escape dismiss):**
  - Row 1 `for teammates` (mono label) → `copy room link` — copies `location.origin + '/?room=' + activeJoin.roomId` (the existing `copyRoomLink` logic ~42300 relocates here); confirmation swaps the row label to `copied` for 1.5s.
  - Row 2 `for guests` → `mint guest link` → `POST /rooms/{id}/guest-links` (existing `mintGuestLinkForRoom` ~50136 relocates + upgrades): result renders IN the popover as a labeled block — mono caption `shown once · expires in 7 days`, the `/g#` URL in a readonly field, a `copy` button (clipboard write + `copied` state). Dismissing the popover clears the token from the DOM (sensitive value never lingers — recon-07). A `manage links` text link opens the guest-links panel (§3.1).
  - **Office interstitial (D4):** when `activeJoin.roomId === 'office'` (or lobby-mount room id is office), `mint guest link` first swaps the popover body to the listen-only consent step (copy in D4, warn-soft accent) with `[mint the link]` / `[cancel]`. Mint proceeds only through the confirm.
- **Topbar:** `#copyRoomLink` (~20438) is retired; the slot renders the current room name (lowercase mono next to the `Room` heading — `the office` / `nbc sync`), sourced from `activeJoinRoomRecord()?.name` with `the office` fallback. The stage meta keeps the meeting title/clock (meeting identity ≠ room identity; both now visible — recon-11 jank fix).

### 3.5 Guest gate + guest terminal states (`/g#token`)

- **Gate = name + green room + join, room named.** On boot with `guestBootToken`, fire `POST /guest/lookup {token}` → `{roomName, live, participantCount}`; render `you're invited to {roomName}` (person text; the existing mode machinery `renderLoginMode` ~24274 already has the string seam ~24307). Invalid/expired token → honest mono state on the gate: `this invite has expired or been revoked.` (no dead join button). Below the name field: the green-room block (§3.2; preview starts only on tile tap or cam-chip-on). `Join as a guest` primary. **`Use Face ID` demoted** to a quiet text link `joining as a member? sign in` that flips to the member login mode (recon-19 jank fix).
- **Terminal state (leave/denied/replaced):** full-screen takeover — the stale landing must NOT render beneath (recon-27 broken issue). Content: mark, `you've left {roomName}.` (from `guestSession.roomName`), one primary `rejoin the room` (re-runs the `/guest/me` resume path ~24436 → green room → join; the guest session cookie is 12h so this works without retyping the name), and nothing else — no Face ID, no member hints, no `Session ended` dead pill. If `/guest/me` 401s (session expired), the button swaps to mono `this session has ended — ask for a fresh link.`
- **Fragment reprocess:** a `hashchange` listener in guest mode re-runs the boot token parse, so re-navigating the same `/g#token` URL in-tab returns to the gate instead of doing nothing (recon-27).
- **Guest mobile tile priority:** in guest sessions the FIRST REMOTE participant takes the hero slot and self drops to the thumbnail strip (invert the current self-hero default inside `updateHearthParticipants`'s hero-slot selection for `guestMode`). Fix the timestamp/pin-button collision on the tile's top-right while in there (recon-25).

### 3.6 Join flow hardening (all principals)

- **Order swap:** `joinRoom` acquires local media BEFORE `openRoomWebSocket()` (today the socket dials first ~30380, so a wedged `getUserMedia` holds a phantom seat — recon-09 broken issue). Media acquisition (`createLocalMediaStream` path ~30792, attempts via `getUserMediaFromAttempts` ~31620) races a **12s watchdog**: on timeout → `setRoomEntryInProgress(false)`, lobby error line `couldn't reach your camera or microphone — check permissions and try again.`, join button → `try again`. No socket ever opens without media, so no phantom `live · 1 inside`. Reconnects are untouched (`roomCanReconnectSignal` ~30457 already requires `localStream`).
- **In-room occupancy honesty:** the stage meta counter includes self — `in room · max(1, n)` while seated (recon-11 `in room · 0`).
- **Status pill dedupe:** one `the room is listening` pill only — keep the topbar pill, remove the floating stage duplicate (recon-11).
- **Meeting bar / chat seam:** with the room chat panel open, center the bar on the shrunken stage: extend the existing `.meeting-bar` width/offset rules (~3078, ~5204 block) with an `.is-room-chat-open` offset equal to the panel width (recon-14).

### 3.7 Archive flow (occupant-aware)

- **Confirm copy names the humans:** `archive "{name}"? {n} people are inside — they'll be disconnected. its record is kept.` (count from `roomsList` `participantCount`; zero-occupant copy stays close to today's). Keep `window.confirm` (a custom dialog is not worth the wave budget).
- **Server (minimal Go, justified):** the archive handler broadcasts a room-scoped `room_closed` event to the room's sockets and ends the sitting (close chain via the existing idle/close seam), so occupants are never marooned in a half-dead room (recon-40). `room_closed` is added to the §6.2 guest write allowlist (it is precisely the event guests must receive).
- **Client:** `room_closed` → `leaveRoom()`; members land on the lobby with a dismissible mono notice `this room was closed.` (the existing calm-chip idiom, see the session_replaced chip ~25355); guests get the terminal card with `this room was closed.` as the message (`guestExitMessage` seam ~24484).

### 3.8 Member leave

`leaveRoom` returns members to the LOBBY (`setActiveTool('room')` state persists; `activeJoin` resets to office per ~49772 — amend: reset to the room just left, so "rejoin" is one click and the lobby doesn't yank selection). No terminal screen for members (home bounce today reads as a glitch; the lobby with the just-left room selected reads as "you stepped out").

## 4. Server changes (minimal Go — exactly three, all additive)

1. **`guestLinkActive` bool on `GET /rooms` rows** — true when the room has ≥1 unexpired, unrevoked guest link (the same predicate as the §7.1 latch's guest-enabled arm). Powers the office listen-only badge honestly. `rooms.go`.
2. **`POST /guest/lookup {token}`** → `{roomName, live, participantCount}`. Public, `websocketOriginAllowed`-checked, rate-limited under the existing `authAttemptAllowedForKeys(["guestjoin", clientIP])` budget, constant-time token validation identical to `/guest/join`, mints nothing, sets no cookie. Added to the §5.3 guest/public allowlist + the W7 route-walk test's exception list. `rooms.go`/`main.go`.
3. **`room_closed` broadcast on archive** — archive handler ends the open sitting through the existing close seam and emits room-scoped `room_closed` to all the room's sockets; event added to the §6.2 guest write-time allowlist. `rooms.go`/`room_live.go`/`main.go`.

Nothing else server-side. Guest-links list/revoke, mint, passcode, archive endpoints all exist (multi-room spec §4.1).
