# The Bonfire Design System

The product is a next-gen agentic meeting room. Keep the current Bonfire direction: warm dark room chrome, parchment board surface, sparse ember accents, Geist UI type, Geist Mono for system labels and numbers, Instrument Serif italic for empty or ceremonial states.

Non-negotiables:

- Product name is The Bonfire.
- Lowercase copy by default except proper nouns and direct action buttons.
- No emoji.
- Ember `#FF7A2B` is used sparingly for live/listening/action states.
- Night surfaces are warm brown-black, not cool grey.
- Board surfaces are parchment (`#FAF6EE`) with warm light shadows.
- Buttons have tactile `scale(0.96)` press feedback.
- Dynamic numbers use tabular numerals.
- The signature motion is three ember halos on a 2400ms clock.
