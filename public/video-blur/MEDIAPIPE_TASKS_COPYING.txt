MediaPipe Tasks Vision — vendored binaries (background-blur "look", card 079)
============================================================================

The files in this directory are unmodified redistributions of Google's
MediaPipe project and its published selfie-segmentation model. They are
vendored (rather than loaded from a CDN at runtime) so the app has no
third-party runtime dependency and the ~9 MB wasm can be cached hard by our
own asset handler. Lazy-loaded only when a user selects the "blur" look.

Vendored files and their upstream sources
-----------------------------------------
  vision_bundle.mjs            @mediapipe/tasks-vision@0.10.14 (npm)
  vision_wasm_internal.js      @mediapipe/tasks-vision@0.10.14/wasm/ (npm)
  vision_wasm_internal.wasm    @mediapipe/tasks-vision@0.10.14/wasm/ (npm)
  selfie_segmenter.tflite      https://storage.googleapis.com/mediapipe-models/
                                 image_segmenter/selfie_segmenter/float16/latest/

Re-derive / verify the exact bytes with:  node scripts/vendor-video-blur.mjs --check
